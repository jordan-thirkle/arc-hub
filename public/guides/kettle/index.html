Open This Build in Planner

Why This Build Works

  • Extremely cheap at 840c for Tier I — affordable after one successful extract
  • Light ammo is the most abundant and cheapest ammunition type
  • Low financial risk — easy to insure and replace
  • Vertical grip eliminates most vertical recoil for accurate follow-up shots
  • Great starter weapon for learning map layouts and extraction points

Pro Tips

  • Pair with an energy drink for infinite sprint stamina while looting
  • Aim for headshots — semi-auto rewards precision over spray
  • Avoid long-range engagements (low bullet velocity makes leading shots harder)
  • Upgrade to Rattler or Tempest as you accumulate wealth
  • Insure Tier I — the 840c buy-in is worth protecting your run

Skill Priority

Allocate points in this order for maximum effectiveness with this build:

  1. Sprint Efficiency (S) — less stamina drain for more uptime
  2. Weight Training (S) — carry more loot per extract
  3. Agile (S) — movement speed helps every engagement
  4. Vitality (S) — extra HP keeps you alive in budget gear
