Why This Build Works

  • Highest single-shot damage in the game at 120 base — one-shots unshielded targets
  • Splash damage clears groups of ARC enemies instantly
  • Heavy Shield provides 80 charge with 52.5% damage mitigation for aggressive play
  • Pressure Regulator augment boosts survivability when brawling at close range
  • Introduced in Patch 1.29.0 alongside Ermal — new meta defining weapon

Pro Tips

  • Reload after every shot — the 1-round magazine means no second chance
  • Use splash damage around corners to flush out campers
  • Pair with a Rattler or Tempest secondary for follow-up clean-up
  • The heavy shield is essential — you will be pushing into close combat
  • Ammo is scarce — carry spare launcher ammo in your quick-use slots

Skill Priority

Allocate points in this order for maximum effectiveness with this build:

  1. Toughness (S) — extra HP for close-range trades
  2. Iron Hide (S) — damage resistance stack with heavy shield
  3. Agile (S) — movement speed for closing distance
  4. Quick Recovery (A) — stamina regen for repositioning
