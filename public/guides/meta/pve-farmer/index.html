Best PvE Farming Builds in ARC Raiders

Role GuidePatch v1.29

PvE Farming builds focus on sustained DPS against ARC enemies, high magazine capacity for extended fights, and utility items for maximum loot extraction. Speed and efficiency are the priorities.

Top Weapons for This Role

#1 — Tempest (Assault Rifle · Medium · 367 DPS)
Tempest II with extended medium mag (31 rounds), angled grip, and scanner augment is the ultimate PvE farming build. Highest AR DPS at 367 with the Scanner revealing loot and enemies through walls.
#2 — Hullcracker (Special · Launcher · 2030 DPS)
Hullcracker I deals 100 damage with 2030 DPS against ARC enemies. Its explosives only detonate against ARC, making it a PvE-specific powerhouse. 5-round mag with fast 20.3 fire rate.
#3 — Torrente (LMG · Medium · 466.4 DPS)
Torrente III with 80-round magazine provides the largest sustained fire capacity for PvE. Crouch for pinpoint accuracy. Excellent for clearing rooms of ARC enemies without reloading.
#4 — Stitcher (SMG · Light · 317.1 DPS)
Stitcher I at only 800c is the budget PvE option. Decent 317.1 DPS and light weight (5kg) leaves plenty of carry capacity for loot.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best PvE weapon in ARC Raiders?

The Tempest II with extended medium mag is the best PvE weapon. Its 367 DPS combined with 31-round magazine and Scanner augment for detecting loot makes it ideal for farming.

What augments are best for PvE farming?

The Scanner augment is the best for PvE farming — it reveals loot containers and enemy positions through walls. Pressure Regulator is a good alternative for combat-focused farming.

What quick-use items should I bring for PvE?

Bring lockpicks for high-value containers, energy drink for sprint stamina, and an advanced medkit for emergency healing. Shield cells help against unexpected PvP encounters.
