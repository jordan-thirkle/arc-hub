Osprey Stealth Sniper

4.5 · Stealth · Osprey TIV

Stay hidden, take precise shots. Osprey IV with silencer, angled grip, stable stock. Signal Jammer augment to avoid detection.

Attachments: Silencer, Angled Grip, Extended Medium Mag, Stable Stock

Augment: Signal Jammer

Shield: Light Shield

Quick Use: stim-shot, painkiller, flashbang, smoke-grenade, bandage

Open This Build in Planner

Why This Build Works

  • Osprey IV one-shots unarmored enemies to the head (45 damage)
  • Silencer keeps you off the minimap and audio range of other players
  • Signal Jammer prevents enemy radar/proximity detection in a radius
  • Stable Stock + Angled Grip make follow-up shots consistent
  • Extended Medium Mag gives 14 rounds instead of 8 for the bolt-action

Pro Tips

  • Relocate after every 2-3 shots — attentive players triangulate your position
  • Flashbang + smoke grenade creates your own cover for rotation
  • Pair with a Burletta II as secondary for close-range defense
  • The silencer reduces bullet velocity; lead your shots more at distance

Skill Priority

Allocate points in this order for maximum effectiveness with this build:

  1. Recoil Compensation (S) — steady aim for second shots
  2. Steady Hands (A) — reduce weapon sway while scoped
  3. Snap Aim (S) — faster scope-in for quick follow-up shots
  4. Light Step (B) — quieter movement between positions
