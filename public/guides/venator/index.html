Why This Build Works

  • Impressive 660 DPS for a pistol — competitive with ARs at close range
  • Two-shot burst mode catches enemies off guard with burst damage
  • Extremely light at 5kg — pairs well with any primary loadout
  • Extended Medium Mag gives 16 rounds for sustained fighting
  • Focus augment tightens shot grouping for the dual-burst pattern

Pro Tips

  • Use as a secondary weapon for close-range backup
  • The two-shot burst is devastating at close range — push aggressively
  • Pairs well with Osprey or Ferro as a CQB sidearm
  • Vertical grip is essential — the burst creates vertical climb

Skill Priority

Allocate points in this order for maximum effectiveness with this build:

  1. Agile (S) — movement speed compensates for pistol range
  2. Nimble Fingers (S) — faster reload in extended fights
  3. Snap Aim (S) — quick swap-to-pistol speed matters
  4. Sprint Efficiency (S) — reposition while swapping weapons
